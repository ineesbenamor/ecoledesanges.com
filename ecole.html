we are a preschool educational approach based on playing, singing, practical activities such as drawing, and social interaction as part of the transition from home to school. we are an educational institutions and learning spaces for children ranging from 2 to 6 years of age, based on a variety of teaching methods.

ACTIVITY

Level 1

We teach children how to make things themselves using simple objects by cutting, coloring and gluing things together until a toy or model is formed.

Teaching drawing to children is easy if proper ways are applied, as up to the age of 3 they are super learners and this is the best time to teach them how to draw.

By playing with others, children learn the art of communication. They come to recognize facial expressions and body language how to express their thoughts and desires in the group game.

Level 2

Every child learns to read at a personal pace. There is no special formula for getting every child to read Children will enjoy the fairy-tale aspect, the exciting battles, and seeing kids on a secret adventure.

Teaching math to children is as easy as 1+1=2. Go beyond pencil and paper to make math a learning experience that's fun. These quick and easy strategies help us teach kids math and will turn them into mini mathematicians.

Songs,Puzzles,Maps and Globes,Map Games,Children's Books Set in Different Countries,Exploring our City or Town.Puzzles are the best when you want a little one to “study” a picture of something for a longer period of time.
